Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Born: July 7, 1981 (Ranchi, Jharkhand, India)

Role: Wicket-keeper Batsman

Achievements:

  • World Cup-winning captain (2011)
  • ICC T20 World Cup winner (2007)
  • ICC Champions Trophy winner (2013)
  • Padma Bhushan, Padma Shri, Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, popularly known as MS Dhoni, is one of the greatest cricket captains in the history of Indian cricket. Under his captaincy, India won several major ICC tournaments including the ICC Cricket World Cup 2011. He is also famous for his calm demeanor on the field and his remarkable finishing abilities with the bat.
